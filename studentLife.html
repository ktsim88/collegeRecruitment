Busch Campus

Busch Campus

Busch Campus is home to all of the STEM classes. The School of Engineering and Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy are homed here. Busch Campus offers the SHI Stadium which hosts Rutgers football. It also offers other recreation facilities and an outdoor art gallery. It is also home to many research centers.

Busch Housing

Livingston Campus

Livingston Campus

Livingston Campus is home to the Rutgers Business School. This campus has a movie theater and many restaurants like Qdoba Mexican Grill. There are a lot of solar panels in this campus. The Asian Americal Cultural Center is housed here.

College Avenue

College Avenue

College Avenue is home to the School of Arts and Sciences, Honors College, School of Planning and Public Policy, and School of Nursing. This campus is very lively with many events being held here. This campus also houses the Zimmerli Art Museum which is an art museum with many collections from Europe and Americas. It's also close to the NJ Transit train station.

Cook & Douglass Campuses

Cook and Douglass Campuses

Technically, they are two seperate campuses but they are directly connected to each other.

Cook Campus houses the School of Enviromental and Biological Sciences and the Rutgers Garden. Douglass Campus houses the Mason Gross School of the Arts and Douglass Residential College which is a womens only college.
