Alumni

Casey Murphy

Murphy played for NCAA Division 1 soccer at Rutger University. She won Big Ten Goalkeeper of the Year and All-American honors. She is a soccer goalkeeper for the North Carolina Courage of the National Women's Soccer League and United States national team.

Sebastian Stan

Stan attened Mason Gross School of the Arts and graduated with a degree in theater. He is known for playing Buck Barnes in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He was also a lead actor in The Falcon and the Winter Soldier and Thunderbolts.

Calista Flockhart

Flockhart attended Mason Gross School of the Arts. She won the Golden Globe Award for playing the main character in Ally McBeal. She was also nominated for Primetime Emmy Award for Ouytstanding Actress in a Comedy Series. She also played Kitty Walker on Brothers & Sisters and Cat Grant on Supergirl.
